# Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis
### Google Data Analytics Capstone Project

## Overview
Analysis of historical trip data from Cyclistic, a bike-share company in Chicago, 
to identify behavioral differences between casual riders and annual members and 
recommend a marketing strategy to convert casual riders into members.

## Business Task
How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?

## Key Findings
- Casual riders take **6.5× longer trips** on average (85 min vs 13 min)
- Casual riders peak on **Sundays** (18,652 rides); members peak on **Tuesdays** (127,974 rides)
- Members ride for **daily commuting**; casual riders ride for **leisure**

## Top 3 Recommendations
1. **Weekend membership plan** — lower-priced plan for Sat/Sun riders
2. **Digital campaigns on Fridays & Saturdays** — target casual riders at peak activity
3. **Cost-savings messaging** — show that 85 min avg rides make annual membership more economical

## Dataset
- Divvy_Trips_2019_Q1.csv — 365,069 rows
- Divvy_Trips_2020_Q1.csv — 426,887 rows
- Source: Motivate International Inc. (public license)
- Total after cleaning: 791,746 rides
